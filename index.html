About this Developer

John Shea is a full-stack web developer, entrepreneur, and investor with a background in law and finance.


In 2012, John founded Proxy Mosaic, a SAAS company that analyzes the link between corporate governance and investment returns.


Prior to founding Proxy Mosaic, John practiced data privacy and security law at DeVore and DeMarco LLP.


John is a bit impatient. He earned his J.D. at age 21, and his B.A. at age 17.

FlareGun

FlareGun (formerly <sos>) is a location-based help app for programmers. Post an SOS request, and a nearby programmer will swoop in on feathery, axe-scented wings to help you in your distress. SOS requests can be tagged with "skills" such as jQuery, Ruby, or MySQL, so as to match up a crisis with the right neck-bearded savior.
